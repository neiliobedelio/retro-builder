HOW TO PLAY

HOW TO PLAY:Use the spacebar to shoot bricks past the LUCHA CABRA! Drag or use keyboard arrows to move.

YOU: "Rapido" the roadrunner is ready to improve Pueblo Viejo, brick by brick. Don’t forget your hard hat—safety first!

LUCHA CABRA: A luchador mask-wearing chupacabra, aka Lucha Cabra, is challenging your progress throughout the process. Booooo, Lucha!

BRICKS: Armed with a never-ending supply of bricks, help improve Pueblo Viejo by hurtling your building blocks.

POWER UPS: Collect the golden $ to earn extra points and blue + to restore Rapido's health.

GOAL: Throw your bricks past the Lucha Cabra to restore and improve Pueblo Viejo. The legendary Lucha Cabra will occasionally summon objects—such as saguaros and javelina—in an attempt to thwart your progress.

PRO TIP: Don’t hit the Lucha Cabra or it will send him into a frenzy!
